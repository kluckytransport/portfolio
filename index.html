About Me

I make games via the Unity Game Engine and I usually work in a team. The role I play in the team is usually the Programmer. I really enjoy programming mechanics for Games. I aspire to try my best to make the mechanics feel good and interactive when the player is playing the game and won't stop until it is exactly how I visioned it in my head.
